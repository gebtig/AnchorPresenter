Privacy Policy for AnchorPresenter Effective Date: 2026 AnchorPresenter (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App. Information Collection AnchorPresenter does not collect, store, or transmit personal information. The App does not upload or transmit these files to any external server. Analytics and Tracking AnchorPresenter does not use: Third-party analytics services Advertising frameworks Tracking technologies Cross-app tracking The App does not track user activity. External Displays The App supports multi-screen output for presentation purposes. No display data is transmitted externally beyond connected local display hardware. Data Security Since AnchorPresenter does not collect or transmit user data, no personal data is stored on external servers. Children's Privacy AnchorPresenter does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.
